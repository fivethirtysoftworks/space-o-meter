Why Space-O-Meter exists

Plenty of system monitors can show disk usage, but most of them bury it in a pile of CPU graphs, fan speeds, memory charts, and network noise. Space-O-Meter strips that down to the one thing a lot of people actually need: immediate storage awareness.

It is built to feel native on macOS, stay out of your way, and make low-space surprises less likely.

What it does best

  • Shows available storage at a glance
  • Surfaces local, external, and mounted network disks
  • Gives you a Stats-inspired look without copying the whole app category
  • Stays small enough to be useful every day
